Well in both cases Best Electronics has succeeded!

Doing some research into the CX52 controller Auxiliary Keypad problems, we discovered some interesting facts:

1. The carbon Dots on Brand New CX52 Auxiliary Keypads had an average Point to Point conductive carbon dot Resistance of between 100 - 200 Ohms.

2. The older (with Time and Use) these conductive Dots on the Auxiliary Keypads got, the contact resistance would go Up. Our tests showed that old CX52 Auxiliary Keypads tested on Used Flex Circuits, with a resistance range over 1,000 to 2,000 Ohms (per conductive dot) would Stop to Work and Not function any more (Not start, pause or reset the 5200 machine)! This Non working condition was mainly caused by General years of use (the surface of the contact carbon dot Glazing over), wear, Human Skins cells and Oils Saturating the carbon conductive dots. Our extensive testing of Old/Used CX52 Auxiliary keypads showed, that resistance values ranged from a (Not start, pause or reset the 5200 machine)! This Non working condition was mainly caused by General years of use (the surface of the contact carbon dot Glazing over), wear, Human Skins cells and Oils Saturating the carbon conductive dots. Our extensive testing of Old/Used CX52 Auxiliary keypads showed, that resistance values ranged from a Low of about 500 Ohms to as High as 5.4+ K Ohms resistance and above.

3. The Size of the Carbon Dots that Atari used in the CX 52 Auxiliary Keypads has varied over the last 15 years! Some of the smallest dots they used were around 4.5 mm in diameter.

The First Improvement Best Electronics tried for and Succeeded with the New replacement tooling was Increasing of the Conductive dot size. We now have in stock Brand New CX52 Auxiliary function pads with Three 6mm Diameter black conductive dots or a 24 - 25% Increase in Conductive Contact Surface Area as compared to the first Auxiliary keypads Atari made! Which should roughly translate into a 24 to 25% Increase in life expectancy for this New Larger Carbon Conductive CX52 Auxiliary keypad.

The Major Upgrade/Improvement that is now in house, is a Brand New Best Electronics version of the CX52 Auxiliary Keypad using Gold Plated Metal Disks (Current State of the Art Technology). Right from the Get Go, these Gold Plated Metal Conductive Disks have a Contact Resistance of 0 Ohms! Our in house tests have shown even after 4,000+ Up/Down, On/ Off cycles (on all three conductive metal disks) that the conduct resistance stayed the same 0 Ohms. Our Manufacture has tested this New Version Auxiliary 5200 Key pad to over 100,000 cycles with No failures! One of the Major Advantages of the Gold Plated Molded in Metal Dots over the Carbon Dot equivalent contacts, is the Metal Disks are Self cleaning (or in worst case, can be cleaned very easily) when you use them with the current Rev. 9 Metal CX52 Flex Circuits! The Only Minor Draw back with these New designed 5200 Best Auxiliary Keypads with the Gold Plated Metal contacts are, they are NOT Recommended for use with the Older CX52 Flex Circuits with the silk-screened black Carbon traces (non metal traces).
