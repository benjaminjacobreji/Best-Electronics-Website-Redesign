FAQ

1. Are the Atari items on your website really in stock?

Yes, I would say that 98 to 99% of the Atari items listed at our Best website are in stock. Occasionally we sell out of an Atari item (1 out of 5,000+ Atari items in stock) and just forget to update the Best Web site for that one Atari item. I understand why people ask this question.Many people have found new internet Atari web sites that list Atari items that are not in stock or only have one of that item that quickly sell out!

2. Are the Atari items listed on your website new and if so, how can that be 35 years later?

Unless otherwise stated (reconditioned, used or loose), all Atari items on our website are New. As we cleared out the 4 to 6 different Atari warehouses over the past 20 years in Sunnyvale California, we bought thousands of pallets of new Atari Products, OEM Atari replacement parts, Atari Accessories, Atari Collector items, Atari Corporate Headquarters items like Prototype Atari products, Atari Engineering items and now have them in our different Atari warehouses. Many Atari items we have in stock we have a lifetime supply of and some more popular Atari items we do sell out of and they disappear into Atari history.

3. Do you really have all of the Atari items they says you have in stock?

Yes a good percentage of our Atari business over the last 35+ years has come from Atari Customer Referrals/Recommendations from past Happy customers and yes all of the Atari items they say we have in stock are really in stock!

4. A friend said you helped him fix his faulty Atari product?

Yes we have been helping Atari owner's fix their Atari products for 27+ years now. To properly help you fix / repair your Atari product, the more information you an give us (E-Mail or Call in) about your Atari product problems, the better the chances we can help you Diagnose and fix your Atari problem the first time.
